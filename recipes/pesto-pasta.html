Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
